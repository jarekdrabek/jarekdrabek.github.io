Production

From AI demo to production system

The model call is usually the easy part. The real work is connecting AI to users, permissions, business data, workflows, APIs, infrastructure, monitoring, and product requirements.

Fragile AI demo

  • Works in a controlled scenario
  • Depends on prompt luck
  • Limited business context
  • Weak integration with real systems
  • No clear monitoring, fallback, or ownership

Production-grade AI system

  • Connected to real workflows
  • Grounded in business data
  • Permission-aware and testable
  • Observable and maintainable
  • Designed for edge cases and launch
